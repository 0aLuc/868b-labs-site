About 868b Labs Inc.

Prior to founding 868b Labs Inc., I spent 20 years building and leading billion-dollar space robotics programs. This experience taught me how to manage complex, mission-critical projects where failure is not an option, and operate at a large scale, always with a pragmatic, security-minded, and outcomes-driven approach.

In 2022, I shifted into Ethereum and applied these principles while leading the Ethereum Foundation’s Ecosystem Support Program, directly overseeing the review of thousands of grant applications, and directing funding to over 350 projects across developer tooling to protocol research. This provided a unique vantage point on the Web3 landscape from the ground up. I learned what makes a project credible, resilient, and sustainable in an ever-evolving ecosystem.
